Turns

  • Wall Dudes is played by two players taking turns.
  • During each turn, a player selects one of their wall dudes and moves it one, two or three times.
  • Whenever a wall dude leaves a tile, that tile becomes a wall.
  • Jumpers can't pass through walls or other wall dudes (belonging to any player).
  • If any of a player's wall dudes has no valid moves at the start of their turn, that player loses.
  • If any of a player's wall dudes starts their turn on a lava tile, that player loses.

Movement

  • Once a wall dude is selected during a players turn, that wall dude moves one, two or three times.
  • During a move, a wall dude travels along a straight line (horizontally or diagonally) until blocked by a wall or another wall dude.
